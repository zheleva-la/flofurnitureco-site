About

A family workshop, built from our own storage problem.

From moving into our home and growing our family, we found our storage solutions needed grow with us.

This is where we started to build made to measure, beautiful pieces of furniture, that helped solved this issue, whilst still ensured our home looked beautiful!

After practicing and refining our skills over the years, we decided it was time for Flo's Furniture to be created so we can bring solid yet elegant pieces of handmade furniture into homes!
