About

A family workshop, built from our own storage problem.

Handmade solid pine furniture for storage solutions — that's the short version. Here's the longer one.

When we moved into our home and our family started to grow, we found that our storage just couldn't keep up. So we started building our own — made-to-measure, solid pine pieces designed to solve the exact problem we had, while still looking like they belonged in the house.

After practicing and refining those skills over a few years, we decided it was time to bring that same approach to other homes — which is where Flo's Furniture Co. came from: solid, elegant, handmade pieces, built to fit.
